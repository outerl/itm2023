AequilibraE tutorial

The AequilibraE tutorial has been produced as a Jupyter notebook that can be run in either your local system or on Google Colab.

This tutorial includes skimming and traffic assignment on a network with over 380,000 links and nearly 700 zones!!

Tradesman Tutorial

The Tradesman tutorial is also available as a Jupyter notebook for use locally or on Google Colab.

In this tutorial, we build a model for Gibraltar, including its network, zoning system and population pyramid, finishing with some cool visualization
